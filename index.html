About Us

Interact District 3292, Nepal

Interact District 3292 is a thriving youth service community under Rotary District 3292 (Nepal & Bhutan). With over 120 clubs spanning school and community environments, we empower thousands of young leaders to build skills, shape legacies, and dedicate themselves to impactful community projects.

Our Mission

To foster leadership, build international understanding, and drive local community service by empowering youth to implement purposeful and sustainable projects.

Our Vision

To develop a generation of empathetic, capable, and united youth leaders whose actions shape a positive legacy for society.

Excellence Shapes Legacy

The theme Excellence Shapes Legacy represents a commitment to pursuing excellence in every action, decision, and service. It inspires every Interactor to lead with integrity, embrace continuous growth, and create a lasting impact that extends beyond their tenure.
